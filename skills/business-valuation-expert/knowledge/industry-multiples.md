## Multiple Adjustment Factors

### Positive Adjustments (+0.25x to +1.0x)
- Strong recurring revenue
- Growing customer base
- Diverse revenue streams
- Low owner dependency
- Strong management team
- Premium brand/reputation
- Proprietary technology/processes
- High barriers to entry
- Real estate included

### Negative Adjustments (-0.25x to -1.0x)
- Customer concentration (>25% from single customer)
- Owner dependency
- Declining revenue
- Industry headwinds
- Poor record quality
- Aging equipment
- Lease expiring soon
- Key employee risk
- Regulatory challenges

## Data Sources

Multiples derived from:
1. DealStats (formerly Pratt's Stats)
2. BizBuySell/BizQuest transaction data
3. IBBA Market Pulse Survey
4. Pepperdine Private Capital Markets Report
5. GF Data
6. PitchBook

Note: Always verify multiples are appropriate for the specific business size, geography, and current market conditions. Multiples can vary significantly based on:
- Business quality
- Growth trajectory
- Market conditions
- Geographic location
- Deal structure (asset vs stock sale)
