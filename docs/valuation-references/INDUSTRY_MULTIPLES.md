# Industry Valuation Multiples Database

## Data Sources & Methodology

This database combines multiple authoritative sources:
- **BizBuySell Transaction Data**: Q1 2021 - Q4 2025 (5-year trailing)
- **NYU Stern EBITDA Multiples**: January 2026 (public company data, adjusted for size)
- **Business Reference Guide**: Industry-specific rules of thumb

**Important**: Small business multiples (SDE) are typically 2-4x, while public company EBITDA multiples are 8-20x. The difference reflects size premium, liquidity discount, and risk factors.

## Multiple Adjustment Guidelines

### Positive Adjustments (+0.25 to +1.0x)

| Factor | Typical Adjustment |
|--------|-------------------|
| Revenue growth >15% annually | +0.5 to +1.0x |
| No customer >10% of revenue | +0.25 to +0.5x |
| Recurring revenue >50% | +0.5 to +1.0x |
| Manager in place (absentee) | +0.25 to +0.5x |
| Long-term lease (5+ years) | +0.25x |
| Prime location | +0.25 to +0.5x |
| Modern equipment (<5 years) | +0.25x |
| Clean financials (audited) | +0.25x |
| Strong brand/reputation | +0.25 to +0.5x |
| Proprietary products/IP | +0.5 to +1.0x |

### Negative Adjustments (-0.25 to -1.0x)

| Factor | Typical Adjustment |
|--------|-------------------|
| Revenue declining >10% | -0.5 to -1.0x |
| Customer concentration >25% | -0.25 to -0.75x |
| High owner dependence | -0.25 to -0.5x |
| Short lease (<2 years) | -0.25 to -0.5x |
| Old equipment (>10 years) | -0.25 to -0.5x |
| Poor location | -0.25 to -0.5x |
| Incomplete financials | -0.25 to -0.5x |
| Pending litigation | -0.5 to -1.0x |
| Environmental issues | -0.5 to -1.0x |
| Regulatory risk | -0.25 to -0.5x |
| Key employee risk | -0.25 to -0.5x |
